Txsports trustless World Cup prediction markets on SolanaParimutuel prediction pools settled by cryptographic proof of the real match result no bookmaker, no oracle, no admin key. Stake native SOL, win the pool.Powered by TxLINE / TxODDS verifiable sports data anchored on Solana.The problemTwo trust problems sit on top of sports betting:Bookmaker trust. Punters (very acutely in markets like Nigeria) don't fully trust that a bookmaker will pay out fairly, or at all. The house sets the odds, takes a margin, and controls settlement.Oracle trust. Every on-chain prediction market (Polymarket, etc.) still depends on a trusted resolver (a multisig or an optimistic oracle like UMA) to decide who won. That resolver is the recurring attack surface and dispute source.Txsports removes both. Bettors pool native SOL on an outcome; the pool is settled only by a TxLINE Merkle proof of the finalised score, verified on-chain by the TxODDS oracle program itself. There is no house edge (parimutuel winners split the pool), no trusted resolver, and the settlement contract has no admin key and no void/cancel instruction.What it doesReal World Cup fixtures and teams (with flags) across two tabs, plus a Hot predictions panel that surfaces the most competitive pick from each:Simulation 20+ past World Cup matches, replayed as live. Bet, watch it play, settle on-chain by real proof.Real · live genuinely upcoming/live fixtures from the feed. Bet for real; markets without odds are locked ("no betting until odds are available").The flow:Browse either tab (or tap a Hot pick) real teams + flags, consensus odds, live status. Concluded real matches drop off automatically.Bet stake SOL (max 0.3) on Home, Draw, or Away; a live payout estimate shows before you commit. Opening a market is permissionless (created lazily on the first bet).Cancel pull any bet for a full SOL refund, any time before the market settles.Watch it play a simulated live feed streams odds + score (real recorded TxLINE data where available, otherwise a synthesised live 1X2 price) to full time.Resolve anyone submits the TxLINE score proof; the contract CPIs txoracle.validate_stat_v2, verifies the Merkle proof against the on-chain daily-scores root, and settles to the proven outcome.Claim winners take their pro-rata share of the whole SOL pool; a receipt modal shows the payout, and a position can't be claimed twice. Void refund.History every action is keyed to your wallet (local by default; Convex when configured).The demo runs on simulated TxLINE feeds real fixtures replayed as live which the track explicitly allows (finished matches can't be bet on live). The settlement is never simulated: every market resolves against a real TxLINE Merkle proof anchored on-chain.The killer property, shown live in the demo: an unprivileged wallet resolves the market, and there is no instruction anywhere that lets the creator (or anyone) void a result, redirect funds, or settle to the wrong side. Escrow model (native SOL)The market PDA is the escrow. place_bet moves lamports bettor → market with a plain System transfer; claim moves lamports market → winner by directly debiting the program-owned market account. Only the staked lamports are ever moved the market's own rent-exempt reserve is untouched, and the sum of all payouts equals total_pool exactly. No SPL token, no mint, no vault, no ATAs.Design / security notesNo admin key. No instruction can void, cancel, or force a result. Resolution is permissionless and gated solely by a valid proof.Deterministic settlement. The winning predicate is derived on-chain from claimed_outcome; the CPI is pure verification against the anchored root.Same-period binding on the home/away goal stats prevents settling on a mixed in-play snapshot.Root-account binding the daily_scores_roots account is checked against the derived TxODDS PDA and program ownership, so an attacker can't pass a look-alike root.Checks-effects-interactions on claim (position marked claimed before the lamport transfer); checked arithmetic throughout; parimutuel payout uses u128 intermediates.The TxL credit token is used only for TxLINE data-auth (per hackathon rules); all wagering value is native SOL.Environment / keysapp/.env (frontend):VITE_REOWN_PROJECT_ID Reown / WalletConnect project id (adds WalletConnect as a secondary wallet option alongside auto-detected Phantom/Solflare).VITE_RPC_URL Solana devnet RPC (a Helius devnet URL is recommended; public devnet is the default).VITE_CONVEX_URL auto-written by npx convex dev; leave blank to run history off local storage only.Prediction history works with zero setup (stored per-wallet in localStorage).
